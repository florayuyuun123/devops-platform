# 🚀 DevOps Academy: The Zero-to-Job Platform

A premium, free, and offline-capable DevOps training center. Built for students who want to bridge the gap between "Learning" and "Job Ready."

> [!TIP]
> **One-Click Copy & Resizable Terminals**: Our new UI allows you to copy any command with a single click and resize your terminal to fit your workflow.

---

## 🎓 The Teaching Mission
We've restructured the entire curriculum using a strict **What / Why / How** pedagogical system:
1. **The Concept (What)**: Simple definitions for core DevOps tools.
2. **Real-world Context (Why)**: How these tools are used at companies like Google, Amazon, and Netflix.
3. **Execution (How)**: Step-by-step hands-on labs in a safe sandbox environment.

---

## 🛠️ The 10-Phase Curriculum
1.  **Linux Fundamentals** — Speak the language of the kernel.
2.  **Networking & Security** — Connect and protect your infrastructure.
3.  **Git & Version Control** — Master the industry standard for collaboration.
4.  **Docker & Containers** — Package apps so they run anywhere.
5.  **CI/CD Pipelines** — Automate your way to high availability.
6.  **Ansible Automation** — Infrastructure as Code (IaC) (Provisioning).
7.  **Kubernetes** — Orchestrate thousands of containers at once.
8.  **Terraform** — Provision multi-cloud resources from a text file.
9.  **Monitoring & Observability** — Find fires before your customers do.
10. **Capstone Portfolio Project** — Build a resume-ready production app.
